Bob's Slow Cooker Braciole

Description

This is a cheap and easy way to make this Italian classic.

Ingredients

  • 2 (26 ounce) jars marinara sauce
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1¼ cups Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak, pounded to 1/4 inch
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 5 slices bacon
  • 1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  • Step 1

    Pour the marinara sauce into the slow cooker and set on High to warm.

  • Step 2

    Combine the eggs and the breadcrumbs in a small bowl. Sprinkle both sides of the

    meat with salt and pepper. Pat the breadcrumb mixture over one side of the flank

    steak, leaving about a one inch border around edges. Top breadcrumbs with the

    bacon slices; sprinkle with shredded cheese. Starting from one long side, tightly roll

    flank steak into a log. Use string or toothpicks to secure the log in 4 or 5 places.

  • Step 3

    Heat oil in a heavy skillet. Sear the stuffed flank steak in the hot oil until well browned

    on all sides, about 10 minutes. Transfer the meat to the warm sauce in the slow

    cooker. Spoon sauce over meat to cover.

  • Step 4

    Turn slow cooker to Low; cook meat until very tender, 6 to 8 hours. Remove

    string/toothpicks before slicing. Serve with marinara.
